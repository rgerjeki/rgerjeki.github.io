An offline desktop workbench for OSINT investigations. It runs a case through one
loop: collect from public, no-login sources (Maigret, holehe, SEC EDGAR,
CourtListener, ExifTool, and more), curate the findings by hand, then export a
formatted, cited PDF or Word report grounded in real reporting standards
(TraceLabs, FATF/FinCEN KYB). Nothing is faked and nothing contacts the subject.
