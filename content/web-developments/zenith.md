An interactive, real-time sky over your exact location, built with Three.js. Real
star positions, a computed Sun, Moon, and planets, the live ISS crossing overhead,
and short briefings written by Google Gemini and narrated by ElevenLabs. Built for
the Dev.to Weekend Challenge.
